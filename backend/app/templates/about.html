About QuizCraft

Vision

Empower every learner to master any topic through AI-powered, engaging, and adaptive quizzes.

Mission

To make learning fun, personalized, and measurable for everyone, everywhere.

Our Story

QuizCraft was born from a passion for making learning smarter and more engaging. Our journey started in 2024, when a group of educators and technologists teamed up to build a platform that combines the power of AI with the joy of gamified quizzes.

  • 2024: Idea & prototype
  • 2025: Public launch & first 1,000 users
  • 2026: Adaptive engine & gamification
  • 2027: Global classrooms & community
